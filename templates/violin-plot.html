Pareto Analysis: How many references represent n % of your sales?

Each point represents one reference (Item Code, Brand) with total sales quantity (Pieces) as y-axis coordinate. They are split in groups representing 10% of cumulative sales from highest to lowest sales.

Dark Blue points represent the top 20% of total sales, while the Purple points represent the lowest 20 % of total sales.

This highlights the business value of the pareto principle and where to focus company resources.
